    <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage"><h1>
<a id="user-content-introduction" class="anchor" href="#introduction" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Introduction</h1>
<p>Fine particulate matter (PM<sub>2.5</sub>) is an ambient air pollutant for which there
  is strong evidence that it is harmful to human health. In the United States, the
  Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is tasked with setting national ambient
  air quality standards for fine PM and for tracking the emissions of this
  pollutant into the atmosphere. Approximatly every 3 years, the EPA releases its
  database on emissions of PM<sub>2.5</sub>. This database is known as the National
  Emissions Inventory (NEI). You can read more information about the NEI at the
  <a href="http://www.epa.gov/ttn/chief/eiinformation.html">EPA National Emissions Inventory web site</a>.</p>
<p>For each year and for each type of PM source, the NEI records how many tons of
  PM<sub>2.5</sub> were emitted from that source over the course of the entire year. The
  data that we use for this assignment are for 1999, 2002, 2005, and</p>
<ol>
  <li>The data is available <a href="https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/exdata%252Fdata%252FNEI_data.zip">here</a>.</li>
</ol>
<h1>
<a id="user-content-goal" class="anchor" href="#goal" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Goal</h1>
<p>The overall goal is to explore the National Emissions Inventory database and see
  what it say about fine particulate matter pollution in the United states over
  the 10-year period 1999-2008.</p>
<h1>

    2008?

  </li>

  </li>
  <li>Of the four types of sources indicated by the <code>type</code> (point, nonpoint, onroad,
    nonroad) variable, which of these four sources have seen decreases in
    emissions from 1999-2008 for Baltimore City? Which have seen increases in
    emissions from 1999-2008?
    <p><a href="/sefakilic/coursera-exdata/blob/master/course_project_2/plot3.png" target="_blank"><img src="/sefakilic/coursera-exdata/raw/master/course_project_2/plot3.png" alt="./plot3.png" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>
